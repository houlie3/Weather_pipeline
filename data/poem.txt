Tomorrow's forecast, a tale to tell,
Birthplace and Aalborg, similar to compel,
10.9 degrees, a high to share,
While Previous Residence lags, with less to spare.
The winds do howl, with speeds so fine,
31 km/h in two, 28 in the other's line.
Precipitation differs, a notable sight,
0.8 mm in two, 3.6 mm in the other's night.

I morgen vil det være dejligt i Birthplace,
med samme vejr som Aalborg, en skøn tilfældighed.
Temperatur og nedbør, ligner hinanden,
men Previous Residence har mere regn, en værre skæbne.
Derfor vil det være hyggeligt at være i Birthplace i morgen.
